Story description

In this story, the player finds themself in an unknown world. There they meet their first companion, Vita, who is part of an organisation called "Shelter". Whats unique about this world is the existance of monsters. They are at war with humans. "Shelter" is a group of half-human, half-monster creatures that don't fit in in either group, and most of them joined "Shelter" to try and find peace between the two. The player has to choose what to do, who to trust, and how to get back to their own world.
